Intermittent fasting

Maikel Thursday, 23 December 2021 dieting

What works for me is:

  • No calories counting so no MyFitnessPal
  • Not eating until 8pm.
  • Start the timer after going to sleep.
  • Avoid dry-fasting. While it might seem faster it's impossible to keep up. Wet-fasting seems faster in practice.
  • Be mindful of what you on the feeding phase.
  • Walking on a treadmill while watching series.
  • Having a workout routine just for cutting.
  • Not planning meals.
  • Coffee in the mornings but not too loaded.
