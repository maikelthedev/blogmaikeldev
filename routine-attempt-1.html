Routine attempt 1

Maikel Thursday, 17 March 2022 diary

  • 4:30 am: Wake up, go to the gym, run there and shower.
  • 6:00 am: Go back home, eat something. After-workout meal.
  • 10:00 am: Breakfast.
  • 2:00 pm: Lunch
  • 6:00 pm: Dinner.
  • 8pm: Go to sleep.

Notes

You could have your meals at a fix hour.
No coffee.
4 meals, maybe 6am, 10am, 2pm, 6pm. That's 1 every 4 hours and a 12 hours fast.
30 grams of protein on each meal.
Every meal should have greens or at least lettuce.
What's the goal? To slowly drift into 14% body fat without losing muscle (body recomp). We know it works by knowing the weight you lift remains the same as today 17/03/2022 in workit.

I need to go out.

New health goals

I don't want more muscle, I want less fat. Keeping the muscle is a priority while losing the fat. You need goals, changes in routine that are sustainable and traceable for progression:

  1. Try a 3 times per week weigh lifting and see if you can still lift the same after 3 weeks.
  2. You shouldn't track weight, when you do you go too quick. You can glance at it.
  3. You shouldn't track body fat even though 14% is the goal.
  4. Track the exercise you do. HIIT and LISS to be specific. Specially LISS.
  5. Your goal every day is to go to sleep exhausted. So the opposite of what you're currently doing.
  6. Calories is all that you should track.

Real questions

  • How do I add cardio when there's nowhere likeable to run in Cádiz?
  • Can I increase my BMR (basal metabolic rate) ?
  • Once you get the key you can put the scooter on the storage room.

Basic Meal Plan

At 2359 calories total it should be divided in:

  • Carbs: 295 grams
  • Fat: 79 grams
  • Protein: 118 grams

That's per meal of 74 carbs, 20 fat, 30 protein when spread in 4 meals.

This basic meal plan has almost the 2359 calories, it falls short by 200 calories.
