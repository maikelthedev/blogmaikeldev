Too much spare time.

Maikel Wednesday, 2 November 2022 diary

What can I do tomorrow Thursday? The only two things I have left are:

  • Appointment at 17:45 to change dressings. Where I shall ask Paloma tricks and tips to do it myself so that I could come to the surgery only once every 2 days instead of everyday.
  • Dentist on Friday at 9:45.

AND if I manage to have a proper plan I either might or might not spend the weekend in Seville after the dentist. There are no available seats on the trains.

I could:

  • Read a book on kindle.
  • Watch another crappy show in Netflix. Which I'm really tired of.
  • Go for a very long walk either on my own or with friends.

Decisions, decisions.....the key thing is to burn energy.
