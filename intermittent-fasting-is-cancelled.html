Intermittent fasting is cancelled

Maikel Saturday, 16 October 2021 dieting

I'm amending today what I do during the cycles because I think intermittent fasting is too hard after a bulking cycle and will just make me give up altogether.

There's not enough proof of any of its benefits. There's as many studies saying it works at helping keep muscle tissue as studies saying the opposite. I think the focus to keep muscle tissue should be exercising while dieting, instead of dieting alone. All literature agrees on that being the key to keep the muscle.

The main reason I hate it is because it is easy to do after you wake up, but hard to do if you start in the middle of the day. My workout is in the morning and obviously I am not giving up food immediately before and after it, which are the best time to absorb all the protein eaten.

I might do, same as today a light intermittent fasting because I don't have to go to the gym. That is, I might stick voluntarily, unplanned, on weekends to I.F. if I have nothing better to do.
