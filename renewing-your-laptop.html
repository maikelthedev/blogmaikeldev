Renewing your laptop

Maikel Monday, 3 May 2021 guides

What I want:

  • 32 GB of DDR4 RAM to run Kubernetes and ditch the server.
  • Thunderbolt to connect the external monitor.
  • Portable so not heavy.
  • IPS monitor.
  • USB-C charging. No more proprietary stupid cables.
  • I want it to cost less than 900 euros.
  • It has to be i386 platform.
  • Comfortable standard keyboard without strange keys arrangement.

Thoughts

  • It'll be very rare to find a system that does this and is less expensive than having a Hetzner one. Specially the RAM size.
  • You'll never really use the second monitor without having a place to put it comfortably.

Actions

  • Bought the Honor one which has almost all of the requirements except 32GB of RAM.
