Once more with feeling: Hello World!

Maikel Monday, 26 June 2023 introductions

Welcome to my blog, where I've consolidated all my previous WordPress and Jekyll posts into one place. My goal now is to reorganize everything and create a coherent structure.

I'm transitioning from maikel.uk to maikel.dev, and this blog will reside at blog.maikel.dev.

So, who am I?

I'm Maikel, a European-based freelance full-stack software engineer and cloud architect. My expertise lies in integrating software solutions and managing the cloud infrastructure they rely on.

Throughout my career, I've successfully transformed ideas from non-technical companies into reality.

Living currently in Seville, I'm actively seeking long-term contracts. I'm determined to demonstrate that #Mastodon and the #Fediverse can be a valuable platform for finding job opportunities or clients.

As a full-stack software engineer and cloud architect, my interests encompass Angular, React, Python, data science, Node.js, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, Redis, Kubernetes, Docker, Helm, Jenkins, Terraform, and Ansible. You can find more information about these topics on the homepage of this site.

When it comes to music, I enjoy progressive trance, lo-fi, and post-rock. As an audiophile, I have a penchant for quality audio and own Sony XM4 headphones (which I use the most), Aftershokz Titanium for running, and Senheisser 599 for optimal sound experience.

On a personal note, I'm gay and haven't married or had children. Although I don't have any pets of my own, I'm particularly fond of cats and have one of my flatmate at home. I identify as an atheist but maintain a tolerant attitude towards religions.

My hobbies include wandering and urban exploration, which means I possess an intimate knowledge of the streets in any city comparable to that of a certified taxi driver.

I'm here to connect with friends, clients, and potential employers.
