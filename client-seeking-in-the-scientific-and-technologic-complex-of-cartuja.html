Client seeking in the Scientific and Technologic Complex of Cartuja

Maikel Wednesday, 11 May 2022 diary

I'm looking for clients in the PCT (Scientific and Techno Park in Spanish) Cartuja and I'm going to describe here what I'm doing exactly for future reference. This could work as a model of what to do in the future, in other places, based in the DRY principle of software engineering.

The first thing I did to make it a bit fun was to have a very long, ride around every street in the area to get a good grasp of the buildings, the locations, the names of the streets, browsing where the main divisions are. I've noticed in some bits it's all medical, other bits of the place are taken by the University of Seville's campus. Not all of the Cartuja island is useful to me then. It seems all that could matter is in the middle section.

I shall not forget about the Stadium, because apart from sport events it has also offices. Even though Wikipedia makes no mention of it. In any case it has its own website. I know there's a company called Inn Offices that rents spaces in the Stadium but they don't seem to have a directory of companies and start ups running with them. I know their website is this.

On the website of the PCT there is a section of jobs here but I'm not very keen on it as I'm not eager to become anyone's employee. I rather stay independent unless it's strictly necessary to change that or someone makes a really enticing offer.

Today I'm doing just a bit of discovery and gathering tasks to do at the end of this post.

The directory of companies is here and it seems they are exactly 493. I shouldn't assume that directory is updated.

The goal

To be re-employed ASAP.

How to measure it?

In time and steps, the number of steps it takes me to get clients and the number of days. The reason to measure it is so that I can compare in the future, methods of client of client gathering. Today is day zero. To gather the number of steps I should work backwards from what works, using these posts as reference.

To do:

  • Check the website of the stadium.
  • Read the vacancies website of the PCT.
  • Everyone around me should know I'm open to opportunities. Make them aware.
  • Read the website of the PCT itself. Find what's useful there.
  • Use Scrapy to download the entire directory. Or just copy and paste the tables, actually.
  • Publish this post.

Distractions:

  • Starbucks as a location to do some work is a double-edged sword. On one side, the air con makes it nice, when it'll be absolutely bloody awful elsewhere, and the constant movement of people keeps me somehow focused. On the other side the feeling of needing to purchase something every hour (not that anyone asks me to do so) makes me never stay here more than 1 hour. What I need is a place where I can stay for at least four hours then continue from home.
  • Friends bothering me through WhatsApp. Maybe I should tell them from X to Y time I'm busy.
  • Had to get an evaporative air cooler. Considering Seville is extra dry, it is the perfect city to use this kind of coolers and save a ton of money in air conditioning.
  • This site isn't working on mobile, the menu button doesn't do anything. Fix the upper left button. Otherwise this blog is unnacesible from the home page.
